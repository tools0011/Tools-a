How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = more compression)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, WebP, or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Improved SEO rankings (Google considers page speed)
  • Saving storage space on your devices
  • Optimizing images for social media and email
